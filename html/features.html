Features

Record Your Fish

The moment you land a fish, a simple 5 step fish entry screen begins the process of recording your fish.

View Your Trips

View your totals on your fishing location and select one of your trips to view in detail.

Track Your Catch Locations

At the press of a button while recording your fish, the exact location of each catch is captured and can be viewed in your Trip Details page

Compare Your Totals By Species

Each catch entered gets automatically aggregated into multiple metrics screens that allow for advanced analysis based on year and specific fishing locations.

Compete With Your Friends

For each angler that joins you on your trips, compare total fish per year by species including an average catch per trip.
