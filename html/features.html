Features

Record Your Fish

A simple 5 step fish entry screen begins the process of recording your fish.

  • Customizable Species dropdown shows the most common fish caught at your location.
  • Reservoir dropdown allows you to choose from your multiple fishing locations.
  • 'Add Location' button captures the exact gps coordinates of your catch.

View Your Trips

View your totals on a specific fishing location and select one of your trips to view in detail.

  • Trips and Total Amounts can be filtered by year or display all-time totals.
  • Clicking on a Trip will take you to an in depth detail page specific to that trip.

Track Your Trip Details

Each recorded trip will have an associated detail page that gives a comprehensive view of the results.

  • Exact locations of each fish caught on a fully zoomable satellite map.
  • Color-coded location pins differentiate the catch locations of each species of fish.
  • Each catch displayed in a sortable table.
  • Key Weather data from the day of the trip is captured and displayed.

Compare Your Totals By Species

Totals for each species caught is aggregated by each of your fishing locations.

Compete With Your Friends

For each angler that joins you on your trips, compare total fish per year by species including an average catch per trip.
